ABOUT ME

I am a senior technical theatre student at the University of Texas. I have a background in music, and an interest in how competitive marching shows overlap with theatre.

Theatre feels like such an inclusive word, encompassing so many creative outlets and artistic styles and allowing those who participate in it to connect with anyone who chooses to interact with the performances. I've always been drawn to how the lighting and sound designs of a play build upon the text. They build the world around the characters and allow the audience to truly feel as though they are in the moment with the actors rather than just watching from the outside. There is a magic in theatre that swirls around the stage to turn make-believe into reality and I strive to draw it out so that others can experience and see it, too.

In my spare time, I compose music, write short stories and plays, play dungeons and dragons, and engage with other art (music, books, paintings, etc.).

ABOUT ME

I am a senior technical theatre student at the University of Texas (expected graduation August 2026).

I am a jack of all trades—a lighting designer, sound designer, playwright, composer, and dungeon master. I have designed for productions of Twelfth Night, Hadestown: Teen Edition, Three Exorcisms, and Six: Teen Edition, among many others. With over 15 years of experience in daydreaming and making things up, I desire to inspire the same in my audiences.

In my spare time, I compose music, write short stories and plays, play dungeons and dragons, and engage with other art (music, books, paintings, etc.).

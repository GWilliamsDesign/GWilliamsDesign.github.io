Sound Design

Twelfth Night (Thunderstorm and Shipwreck)
A thunderstorm made in QLab for the opening of the play. The director added the shipwreck prologue from the Tempest and this played over that scene.

Original Compositions

Cogs, Circuits & Cords
The music was composed in MuseScore and the mechanical rhythm and distortion added in Logic. It is a piece about AI overtaking memories of music–machinery replacing melodies.
Epic of Alum Act 1
The music was composed in MuseScore and the additional sound effects added in Audacity. This piece retells the first third of a Dungeons & Dragons campaign, the instruments tied to specific characters, and melodies to story beats.
Spirited Winds
The music was composed in MuseScore. It is a piece about freedom, joyous moments, and connection.
Tin Whistle Duet
The music was composed in MuseScore in 15 minutes as part of a class assignment.
Wildflowers
The music was composed in MuseScore. This piece is meant to evoke imagery of wandering through a field of flowers, hands skimming over their petals as clouds drift in front of the sun and a gentle breeze rustles blades of grass.
